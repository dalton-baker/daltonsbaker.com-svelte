Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

This privacy policy applies to all websites and projects operated by Dalton Baker, including but not limited to personal portfolio pages, experimental apps, and side projects. This is a general catch-all policy for small or experimental sites that don’t collect much — if any — data.

Information We Collect

Most of my sites do not collect personal information directly. However, certain hosted services (like analytics or embedded content) may automatically collect basic technical data such as your IP address, browser type, or pages visited.

Use of Information

Any data collected is used only to improve site performance, monitor traffic, or fix problems. I don’t sell, rent, or share your data with anyone, and frankly, I wouldn’t want your browsing habits anyway.

Cookies

Some sites may use cookies for simple session handling or analytics. You can block them in your browser settings without breaking much (if anything).

Third-Party Services

Some projects may use third-party integrations like Cloudflare, GitHub Pages, Azure, or Google Analytics. These services have their own privacy policies, and they might collect limited technical data on their behalf.

Contact

If you have privacy concerns or want something removed, you can reach me at dalton.s.baker@gmail.com.
